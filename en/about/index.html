GKBM LTD is a specialized maritime service provider offering comprehensive solutions in the subjects of dry cargo chartering, vessel management, and maritime finance. With a deep understanding of global shipping markets and a commitment to operational excellence, we support our clients in navigating the complexities of maritime transport with confidence and efficiency.

Our dry cargo chartering division specializes in bridging the needs of cargo owners with the most appropriate vessels, offering bespoke solutions that are finely tuned to prevailing market dynamics. Leveraging deep industry insights, real-time market intelligence, and a global network of shipowners and brokers, we craft chartering strategies that optimize both cost-efficiency and logistical performance. Whether for spot fixtures, time charters, or long-term contracts, our team ensures precise vessel-cargo matching, risk mitigation, and value maximization for every shipment. By aligning commercial acumen with operational reliability, we deliver a seamless and competitive chartering experience tailored to our clients' unique trade requirements.

In parallel, our comprehensive vessel management services are designed to maximize the technical integrity and operational efficiency of each vessel under our care. This includes proactive maintenance planning, performance monitoring, and real-time issue resolution to minimize downtime and ensure reliability at sea. By implementing industry best practices and leveraging advanced management systems, we not only protect the long-term value of our clients’ assets but also ensure full compliance with international maritime regulations, safety protocols, and environmental standards. Our approach delivers peace of mind to shipowners by aligning vessel operations with strategic business and regulatory objectives.

Complementing our core operational and commercial services, our maritime finance division delivers strategic, structured advisory solutions that empower clients to navigate the complexities of capital investment in the shipping industry. With a deep understanding of both shipping markets and financial instruments, we support shipowners, investors, and operators in securing optimal funding structures for fleet expansion, newbuild projects, refinancing or asset acquisitions. By integrating financial insight with a commercial mindset, we help clients achieve long-term, sustainable growth while enhancing asset value and mitigating risk across market cycles.

Driven by integrity, professionalism and results, GKBM LTD is your trusted partner in achieving long-term maritime success.
