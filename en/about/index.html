About

GKBM LTD is a specialized maritime service provider offering comprehensive solutions in the subjects of dry cargo chartering, vessel management, and maritime finance. With a deep understanding of global shipping markets and a commitment to operational excellence, we support our clients in navigating the complexities of maritime transport with confidence and efficiency.

Our dry cargo chartering division connects cargo owners with suitable tonnage through tailored, market-driven strategies.

In parallel, our vessel management services ensure optimal technical and operational performance, safeguarding both assets and compliance.

Complementing our core offerings, our maritime finance expertise provides structured advisory and support to facilitate sustainable growth and investment in the shipping sector.

Driven by integrity, professionalism and results, GKBM LTD is your trusted partner in achieving long-term maritime success.
