GKBM LTD respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

We use Google Analytics to gather anonymous data about how visitors interact with our website. This may include:

  • Pages visited

This data is collected through cookies, which are small text files stored on your device.

2. Use of Cookies

We use cookies solely for the purpose of improving our website and understanding user behavior. Specifically, we use:

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. For more details on how Google uses data collected via cookies, please visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties. Google may process anonymized data according to its own privacy policies.

4. Your Rights

As a website visitor, you have the right to:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics using this browser add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
